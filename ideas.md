## Approach 3: "Superhero Command Center" — Bold, Dynamic, Character-Driven
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Comic-Book Meets Premium Digital — Bold, kinetic, character-first
**Core Principles:**
- The mascot IS the brand — he appears in multiple poses throughout the site
- Dark navy/black backgrounds with explosive gold and electric blue accents
- Bold geometric shapes, diagonal cuts between sections
- Every section has a "Mr. Sweeps says" voice — he narrates the entire experience

**Color Philosophy:** Deep space navy (#0B1F3B) as the universe; gold (#D4AF37) as the hero's power; electric blue (#1BAAFF) as the energy field. The palette mirrors the mascot's own color scheme.

**Layout Paradigm:** Diagonal section dividers. Hero section is full-bleed with mascot dominating the right side. Casino cards have a slight tilt/perspective effect. The mascot floats in a sticky sidebar on desktop.

**Signature Elements:**
- Animated coin rain in the hero background
- "Mr. Sweeps says..." speech bubbles throughout the page
- Neon glow on all interactive elements
- Diagonal section transitions

**Interaction Philosophy:** The mascot reacts to scroll — he "flies in" from the side. Cards have a 3D tilt on hover. CTAs have a pulse glow effect.

**Animation:** 
- Hero: Coins rain down continuously (CSS keyframes)
- Mascot: Float animation (up/down, 3s ease)
- Cards: Slide up on scroll (IntersectionObserver)
- Section transitions: Diagonal cuts with clip-path
- Neon borders: Animated gradient border on hover

**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed (display/hero) + DM Sans (body/cards) — condensed bold for impact, clean sans for readability
</text>

## CHOSEN APPROACH: Approach 3 — "Superhero Command Center"

The mascot is the star. He narrates, guides, and energizes the entire experience. The dark navy/gold/electric blue palette mirrors his costume. Diagonal section cuts create dynamism. This approach best fulfills the brief: "the character takes the lead voice of Sweeptakes.com."
